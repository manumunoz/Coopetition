In this part of the study you will participate in an encryption task.

You will be shown a three-letter word and an encryption table on the screen. In the encryption table, each letter of the alphabet is matched with a unique 3-digit number.

Your task is to encrypt each letter of the word by typing the correct 3-digit number below it.

You will have two minutes to encrypt as many words as possible.

Your Bonus 1 will be determined as follows: for every correct word you will get {{ Constants.correct_pay }} {{ Constants.exp_currency }} .

There will be two rounds:

  • The first is a training round to familiarize you with the task. This will not affect your Bonus 1.
  • The second is a scoring round, in which you will be paid for each correctly encrypted word.
See the example below:

Example (Part 1).

In this example, the word shown on the screen (Figure 1) is NKW. The encryption table on the bottom shows that

  • N is matched to 332.
  • K is matched to 717
  • W is matched to 164
After you identify the correct 3-digit numbers, enter them in the boxes below the letters. Before you enter the number, the box will initially display a zero.

Example (Part 2).

The correct screen should look like the one displayed in Figure 2.

Figure 2.

After clicking on the “Submit” button, a new randomly formed word will appear on the next screen for you to encrypt. Also, a new code 3-digit number will be generated for each letter.

The computer will count the number of words you encrypted correctly. You will be informed of your score of correct answers at the end of the study. {# This number is shown on the top of the screen.#}

Note that the remaining time is shown in the upper right left of the screen.

Tip: You can easily switch between the boxes to enter the numbers by using the “tabulator” key on your keyboard.
If you understood the instructions please click on the continue button.
