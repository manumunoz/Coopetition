In Part 1 of the study you will carry out an encryption task as follows:

You will be shown three-letter words as illustrated in Figure 1. On the bottom of the screen, you will see an encryption table where each letter of the alphabet is matched with a unique 3-digit number.

Your task is to encrypt each letter of the word by typing the correct 3-digit number in the box below it.

Once you are done click on the submit button and a new word will be displayed. For each word, a new encryption table will be randomly generated.

You will have two minutes to encrypt as many words as possible. Note that the remaining time is shown in the upper right left of the screen.

Your Bonus 1 will be determined as follows: for every correct word you will get {{ Constants.correct_pay }} {{ Constants.exp_currency }} .

In Part 1 there are two stages:

  • The first is a training stage to familiarize you with the task. This will not affect your Bonus 1.
  • The second is a scoring stage, in which you will be paid for each correctly encrypted word.
See the example below for more clarity:

Example (Part 1).

In this example, the word shown on the screen (Figure 1) is NKW. The encryption table on the bottom shows that

  • N is matched to 332.
  • K is matched to 717
  • W is matched to 164
After you identify the correct 3-digit numbers, enter them in the boxes below the letters. Before you enter the number, the box will initially display a zero.

When the time ends, the computer will count the number of words you encrypted correctly. You will be informed of your score of correct answers at the end of the study.

Tip: You can easily switch between the boxes to enter the numbers by using the “tabulator” key on your keyboard.
If you understood the instructions please click on the continue button.
