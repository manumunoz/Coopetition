The first task is carried out individually, it is an encryption task. Your aim is to encrypt each letter of the word by typing the correct 3-digit number in the box below it. This is explained below:

See the example below for more clarity:

Example Task 1.

In the encryption task, you will be shown three-letter words. The example in Figure 1 shows the word SXG.

On the bottom of the screen, you will see an encryption table where each letter of the alphabet is matched with a unique 3-digit number. The encryption table on the bottom shows that

  • S is matched to 403.
  • X is matched to 429
  • G is matched to 383

Figure 1.
Example (Part 2).

After you identify the correct 3-digit numbers, enter them in the boxes below the letters. Before you enter the number, the box will initially display a zero.

The correct screen should look like the one displayed in Figure 2.

Once you are done click on the submit button and a new word will be displayed. For each word, a new encryption table will be randomly generated.

You will have two minutes to encrypt as many words as possible. Note that the remaining time is shown in the upper right left of the screen.

Tip: You can easily switch between the boxes to enter the numbers by using the “tabulator” key on your keyboard.

Figure 2.
Bonus 1: Individual task.

When the time ends, the computer will count the number of words you encrypted correctly. For your Bonus 1, you will get {{ Constants.correct_pay }} {{ Constants.exp_currency }} for every correct word.

You will be informed of your score of correct answers at the end of the study.

Note: Before task 1 you will have a 60 second trial where you can familiarize with the encryption task. The score of the trial does not add to your bonus. After the trial you will participate in Task 1, for which you will have 2 minutes.
If you understood the instructions please click on the continue button.
