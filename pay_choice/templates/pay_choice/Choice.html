Scheme 1:

Each participant will be paid their individual score plus half of the sum of both players scores.

For example: YOU scored 10 and the OTHER scored 12 correct words. Half of the sum of both playersâ€™ scores is (10+12) / 2 = 11. Then

  • YOU receive (10 + 11) = 21 points.
  • The OTHER player receives (12 + 11) = 23 points.






Scheme 2:

The participants with lowest score will be paid a bonus equal to his score. The participant with the highest score will be paid a bonus equal to three times his score.

For example: YOU scored 10 and the OTHER scored 12 correct words. The OTHER participant has the highest score. Then:

  • YOU receive 10 points.
  • The OTHER player receives (12 * 3) = 36 points.
In case of a tie, the computer will randomly choose between YOU and the OTHER. The chosen participant will be paid 3 times his score and the participant not chosen will be paid his score.
